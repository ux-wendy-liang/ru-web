Case Study Section 背景规则

哪些 section 加浅灰背景，哪些保持白色——交替使用，让页面有节奏感

Section 背景 原因
Hero（标题、封面、项目信息） 白色 干净开场，让封面图做视觉主角
The Problem 白色 正文阅读区，保持简洁
Key Design Decisions 浅灰 #F8F9FA 分析思考区域，中性灰让内容说话
Solution / Design 白色 图片多的区域，白色让截图更清晰
Impact（数据卡片） 浅青 #F6FAF9 成果展示——青色呼应品牌，暗示「成功、增长」
Testimonial / Quote 浅暖 #FAF9F7 人的声音用暖色——温暖、真实、可信
What I Learned 浅暖 #FAF9F7 个人反思，暖色调传递成长和温度

规则总结：三种背景色按内容性质搭配使用——
🤍 白色：默认背景、图片多的区域
🩵 浅青 #F6FAF9：品牌/成果相关（Impact）
🩶 浅灰 #F8F9FA：分析思考区（Design Decisions）
🤎 浅暖 #FAF9F7：人文温度区（Quote、Reflection）

The Problem

Why the Old Dashboard Didn't Work

LinkedIn's internal data teams relied on three separate tools to visualize and analyze data, leading to fragmented workflows and duplicated effort.

Challenge: Users spent 40% of their time switching between tools just to get a complete picture of their data.

Key Decisions

Research-Driven Design Choices

Through user interviews and data analysis, I identified three core principles that would guide the redesign.

Insight: Power users and casual users had fundamentally different mental models for data exploration.
Solution: Role-based default views that adapt to each user type while remaining fully customizable.
